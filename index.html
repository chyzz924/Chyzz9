Photographer

To me, photography is the art of observing and waiting. I’m drawn to quiet, fleeting moments—especially when capturing animals in the street or in nature. There’s something raw and honest in their gaze that fascinates me. Through the lens, I’ve learned to slow down and appreciate both the chaos and calm around us. I believe every photo has a story, and I hope my work reflects the quiet beauty in the everyday.

Artist

Drawing, to me, is a quiet form of expression. Whether it’s the soft shading of a pencil sketch or the pacing and emotion in a comic panel, I enjoy building worlds and characters on paper. Though I’m an amateur, I love turning ideas into lines and telling stories through images. I hope my work makes people pause and feel the warmth and life behind each scene.
